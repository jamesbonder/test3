A little bit about me

I apply knowledge and skills of the latest standards and technologies to create powerful user focused websites. I specialise in front-end web development but also have been involved in the development of other forms of digital media including Graphic Design, and Video Production/Editing.

I develop and create responsive, performance driven websites that work cross-platform and on a wide range of devices and form factors. My development skills include HTML5, CSS3, JQuery, SASS/LESS, SVG, Wordpress and Skeleton. I am currently working on learning JavaScript and PHP to broaden my range of skills. As a designer, I use tools such as Photoshop and Illustrator alongside technologies such as SVG to bring design to life on the web.
